Short Talks

Mon, Sep 11

  • Adrian Martini (Univ. Oxford) - Large Deviations for Paracontrolled Solutions to Stochastic PDEs
  • Abdulwahab Mohamed (Univ. Edinburgh) - Rough Uhlenbeck compactness and additive functions
  • Tal Orenshtein (Univ. Milano-Bicocca) - Rough walks

Tue, Sep 12

  • Mo Dick Wong (Univ. Durham) - Weyl's law for Liouville quantum gravity
  • Léonie Papon (Univ. Durham) - Level lines of the massive Gaussian free field
  • Harry Giles (Univ. Warwick) - Self-repelling Brownian polymers in the critical dimension

Wed, Sep 13

  • Ulrik Hansen (Univ. Fribourg) - The uniform even subgraph and the Ising model
  • Gideon Chiusole (TU Munich) - Towards abstract Wiener model spaces
  • Weile Weng (TU Berlin) - Quenched LLTs for RWRE admitting bounded cycle representation
  • Lukas Gräfner (Free Univ. Berlin) - Scaling-supercritical SDEs w. nearly divergence-free distributional drift
  • Guilherme de Lima Feltes (Univ. Münster) - A priori bounds for 2d gPAM
  • Levi Haunschmidt-Sibitz (TU Vienna) - Diffusion in the curl of the Gaussian free field
