I am Professor of Mathematics (Probability and Statistics) at the University of Milano-Bicocca in the Department of Mathematics and Applications

My research focuses on probability theory and its applications, including statistical mechanics (disordered systems, random polymers), stochastic equations (singular PDEs, scaling limits), and random walks (asymptotic properties, renewal theory)

I am a member of the faculty board of the joint PhD Program in Mathematics between the universities of Milano-Bicocca, Pavia, Cattolica del Sacro Cuore and INdAM

I currently serve as Associate Editor of Annales de l'Institut Henri Poincar√©
